My RSI Story

Web Sites

  • ScanSoft Dragon NaturallySpeaking - The official purchase and support site for NatSpeak.
  • NaturallySpeaking Unofficial Information Pages - Joel Gould was the lead architect of NatSpeak and the developer of NatLink. This is his site, including pages on NatLink and useful information about NatSpeak. No longer being updated, but still useful.
  • NatLink-related utilities developed by NatLink users - A collection of useful NatLink voice commands coordinated by Jonathan Epstein. Vocola coexists happily with these.
  • Kim Patch's Macros - A large set of macros for NatSpeak Pro 5 (free) or 6 ($24). Full of good voice command ideas, many of which are reflected in the sample Vocola commands.
  • Speech.squidnet.com - Scott Weinstein's Speech Recognition WIKI, including a NatLink + Vocola installer.
  • SpeechUtilities.com - David Austin's site, with help for getting the most out of the NatSpeak macro languages. If you decide to stick with these, get here the superior command browser "SayWhat".
  • Ruth Rose's NatSpeak Site - Useful suggestions for getting the most out of NatSpeak
  • Computing Out Loud - Susan Fulton's general-purpose voice recognition site with information and links.
  • Windows Keyboard Shortcuts - Microsoft site with thorough description of keyboard shortcuts by Windows version or product.
  • Typing Injury FAQ - Resources for injured typists.

Mailing Lists

  • VoiceGroup - General discussion of voice recognition issues, mostly NatSpeak related. Lots of traffic.
  • VoiceCoder - Good quality list for programmers using voice recognition.
  • NatPython - NatLink discussion, low-volume with some Vocola traffic.
  • NatPro Macro Writers - Discussion of NatSpeak macros, particularly advanced scripting commands.
  • ScanSoft Public Forum - Public NatSpeak discussion list at ScanSoft.
